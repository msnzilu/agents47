ðŸ’¡ What are Multi-Agent Orchestrations?

Orchestrations allow you to coordinate multiple AI agents to work together on complex tasks. Each agent can specialize in different aspects of the problem, and the orchestrator coordinates their efforts.

  • Sequential: Agents execute one after another
  • Parallel: Multiple agents work simultaneously
  • Conditional: Agents execute based on conditions
  • Hierarchical: Agents organized in a tree structure
